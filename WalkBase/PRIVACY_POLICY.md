# Privacy Policy — Walk Track

**App:** Walk Track (`com.codesandchips.walktrack`)
**Developer:** _[your developer / publisher name]_
**Last updated:** 22 June 2026

## Summary

Walk Track is private by design. It has **no internet access**, so your information
physically cannot leave your device. There is **no account, no cloud, no analytics,
no advertising, and no tracking**. Everything the app knows about you stays in the
app's own storage on your phone.

## Information the app uses

All of the following is stored **only on your device** and is never transmitted:

- **Step count** — read from your phone's on-device step-counter sensor to award
  in-game "Bricks". This requires the **Physical activity** permission
  (`ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION`).
- **Your town and game progress** — buildings, levels, currency, streaks and daily
  step history, kept in a local database inside the app.
- **Optional height and weight** — if you choose to enter them, they are used only on
  your device to estimate walking distance and calories. They are never sent anywhere.
- **Preferences** — settings such as your daily goal, town name and toggles.

The app does **not** collect or use your location/GPS, contacts, photos, microphone,
camera, or any device identifiers.

## Permissions and why they are needed

- **Physical activity (`ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION`)** — to read your step count from the
  device sensor. This is the only permission tied to personal data.
- **Notifications (`POST_NOTIFICATIONS`)** and **foreground service
  (`FOREGROUND_SERVICE` / `FOREGROUND_SERVICE_HEALTH`)** — to keep counting your steps
  while the app is in the background, shown via an ongoing notification. No data is
  transmitted; the foreground service only reads the step sensor.
- **Vibrate (`VIBRATE`)** — optional haptic feedback on in-app actions.

The app deliberately does **not** request `INTERNET` or `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE`.

## Data sharing

None. The app shares no data with the developer or any third party, because it has no
network access.

## Data retention and deletion

Your data lives only in the app's on-device storage. You can:

- **Back it up yourself** via *Settings → Data → Export*, which writes a file you
  control (you choose where it goes through the system share sheet).
- **Delete everything** by uninstalling the app, which removes all of its data. Nothing
  is retained anywhere else.

## Children

Walk Track contains no ads, no purchases, no user-generated content and collects no
data, and is suitable for all ages.
